Codes Checked

Every parameter validated against the code you select.

IBC 2021

Commercial buildings. Riser 4"–7", tread min 11", width 44", headroom 6'-8", max flight rise 12'.

IRC 2021

Residential buildings. Riser max 7.75", tread min 10", nosing 3/4"–1.25" required, max flight rise 12'-7".

ADA 2010

Accessible design. Riser 4"–7", tread min 11", nosing max 1/2" (beveled), 60" landing for wheelchair turning.

This tool checks dimensional compliance only. It does not verify structural load capacity, fire ratings, material specs, or local code amendments. Always consult your local building authority before building.
